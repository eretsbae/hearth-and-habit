**Hearth & Habit** is a practical guide to running a home without letting it run you.

We write for busy people across the United States and Canada — homeowners, renters, first-apartment folks — who want their living space to work well without turning home care into a hobby. No aspirational makeovers, no $400 organizing hauls. Just clear, tested guidance on the stuff that actually keeps a household going.

## What we cover

- **Home Maintenance & Repairs** — seasonal upkeep, small fixes, and knowing when to call a pro
- **Cleaning & Organization** — realistic routines and systems that survive real life
- **Energy & Utility Savings** — cutting your bills with habits and smart upgrades
- **Kitchen & Food Habits** — meal planning, appliance care, and wasting less food
- **Yard & Outdoor Basics** — the bare minimum that keeps the outside of your home in shape

## Our approach

Every guide follows the same rules: explain the *why* in plain language, give steps you can actually follow this weekend, and be honest about when a task is worth hiring out. We favor evergreen advice over trends — a good furnace-filter routine matters every single year.

Hearth & Habit uses modern editorial tools, including AI assistance, in producing and illustrating its guides. Every article is built around established, verifiable home-care practices.
